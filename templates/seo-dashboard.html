Content inventory by vertical. Every published page grouped by industry and content type. Each page lists its target keywords with Ahrefs metrics and cannibalization flags (orange = medium, red = high). Keyword cannibalization alerts. When 2+ pages target the same keyword, they compete against each other in Google instead of reinforcing one winner. High risk (red) = 3+ pages on one keyword; Medium (orange) = 2 pages. Look for: high-volume keywords with conflicts (biggest ranking losses), and decide per case whether to merge pages, redirect, or change the secondary page's target keyword.
