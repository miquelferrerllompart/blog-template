# Writing Style — Examples

## Pillar 1: Direct and unambiguous

**Good:** "Set up once. See results in a week."
**Bad:** "Frictionless omnichannel engagement solution."

## Pillar 3: Audience-first

**Good:** "Reach your customers where they already are, without paying for ads."
**Bad:** "Push notifications."

**Good:** "Your brand in the place your customers check fifty times a day."
**Bad:** "Branded digital cards."

## Pillar 4: Aspirational without grandiosity

**Good:** "Your physical store becomes a data point, not a blind spot."
**Bad:** "We will revolutionise retail."

## Concrete over abstract

**Good:** "Drives 3x more redemptions than email-only programs."
**Bad:** "Improves engagement."

## Active voice

**Good:** "Automated workflows deliver 90% open rates."
**Bad:** "90% open rates are delivered by automated workflows."
